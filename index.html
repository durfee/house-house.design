House House is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Vancouver, Canada. We can help you build intelligent, purposeful products, websites and apps.

We are different than the last agency you hired. You see, we love how design can help people. Other design shops shoehorn your business into their process – we listen and help you grow. We’re not interested in adding to the noise — that doesn’t help anyone. We're here to cut through the cacophony and build good looking products with purpose and function.

To do this, we listen to your story. In discovering who you are, we uncover who is at the heart of your business. We work until we can connect your goals to their needs in a way that is seamless, accessible, and intelligent. We work until we know your audience as well as your dog knows its bone. We work until they come away saying, “of course.”

You’ve got a visionary idea or you wouldn’t be here right now. Let’s start a conversation to see if our values jive with your goals. To see if our approach fits your needs. If our solution-focused, scalable work meshes with your vision.

Hire us and let's talk. ✌️
